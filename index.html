Unicef's Program in RDC

This project specifically targets children affected by armed militia violence in the Democratic Republic of Congo. It consists of transitional care for war-affected children and the implementation of pilot programmes for socio-economic integration in urban areas. UNICEF has set a main objective which consists of coordinating local actors in North Kivu. In addition, UNICEF is trying to help 80% of the targeted children to benefit from protection measures in the region. You'll discover more about Unicef's program and our team's action below.
